Executive Summary

SentraIQ is an AI-powered Evidence Lakehouse platform that automates compliance evidence collection, mapping, and audit preparation for regulated industries. By leveraging OpenAI GPT-4 and advanced data lakehouse architecture, SentraIQ reduces audit preparation time by 80% while ensuring tamper-proof, cryptographically-verified evidence chains.

What is SentraIQ?

SentraIQ is a hybrid Evidence Lakehouse that combines the flexibility of data lakes with the structure of data warehouses, specifically designed for compliance and audit evidence management. It automates the entire evidence lifecycle from ingestion to audit pack generation.

Three-Layer Architecture

01 Raw Vault
  • Immutable storage of raw logs and documents
  • SHA-256 cryptographic hashing for tamper detection
  • Supports JSON, CSV, Syslog, PDF, DOCX, TXT
  • Automated metadata extraction and timestamping
02 Dojo Mapper
  • Automatic mapping of evidence to regulatory controls
  • PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST 800-53, SWIFT CSP
  • AI-driven relevance scoring (0-100% confidence)
  • Natural language evidence search using OpenAI GPT-4
03 Assurance Pack
  • One-click compliance pack generation
  • Tamper-proof bundles with cryptographic verification
  • Audit-ready documentation with evidence lineage
  • Support for date-range filtering and multi-frameworks

Strategic Use Cases

Use Case 1: PCI-DSS Compliance

Problem: Manual evidence collection across 300+ controls takes 200+ hours per audit cycle.

SentraIQ Solution: Ingest firewall/access logs; AI automatically maps them to PCI-DSS 4.0 requirements (e.g., 8.2, 10.2). Generate audit packs in under 5 minutes.

Result: 180 hours saved, 100% control coverage.
Use Case 2: SWIFT CSP Audit

Problem: Banks must prove quarterly compliance. Logs scattered across multiple systems require extensive manual analysis.

SentraIQ Solution: Ingest SWIFT access/MFA logs. Query: "Show privileged access in Q4". AI returns relevant logs with 95%+ accuracy.

Result: Quarterly prep reduced from 3 weeks to 2 days.
Use Case 3: Security Incident Response

Problem: Compliance teams must prove procedures were followed and proper logging maintained during an incident.

SentraIQ Solution: Query for specific IP attempts and timeframes. AI retrieves logs across Firewall, Access, and SIEM with full timeline and cryptographic proof.

Result: Documentation completed in 30 minutes vs. 8 hours.
Use Case 4: SOC 2 & ISO 27001 Certification

Problem: Continuous evidence collection is error-prone. Pursuing ISO 27001 involves 114 controls across 14 domains.

SentraIQ Solution: Continuous ingestion of App/Access/Backup logs. Automated mapping to Trust Services Criteria or Annex A controls. Real-time coverage dashboard.

Result: Certification achieved 6 months earlier than planned.
